Rushes is a non-profit exhibition space in St Kilda, run by artists, art workers and curators. We are dedicated to providing a platform to showcase contemporary video art from both Australian and international artists. We look forward to working with different artists, in all stages of their careers to explore, experiment and take risks with their video practices, within the public realm. 

The location of our screen offers an interesting opportunity to present art to a wider audience and grow stronger connections between art and non-art communities, in the hope of starting new, critical and conceptual dialogues between unexpected viewers and casual passers. 



We would like to acknowledge Traditional Custodians of the land on which Rushes operates, the Boonwurrung people, and pay our respect to their Elders, past, present and emerging.

Rushes is generously supported by the City of Port Philip’s Love My Place program and BAU Brearley Architects + Urbanists.
